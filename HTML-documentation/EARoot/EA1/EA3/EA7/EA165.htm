-- Definition --<br /></p><p>Unit for dissemination or use of statistical information that is represented as a grid cell.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>-- Description --<br /></p><p>A statistical grid cell is associated with:<br /></p><p>- the unique statistical grid cell of its upper level (if any) it is covered by,<br /></p><p>- the statistical grid cells of its lower level (if any) it covers.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>NB Statistical grid cells are squares.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>EXAMPLE In a quadtree structure, some cells are associated with the four cells they are decomposed into.<br /></p>
