The generic administrative unit application schema. <br /></p><p>This includes administrative unit types that are not modeled in a more specialized schema. In many cases these manage specific administrative functions as delegated by a government unit, such as<br /></p><p>- catchment management authorities<br /></p><p>- school zones<br /></p><p>- police and law enforcement<br /></p><p>- fire and emergency services<br /></p><p>In other cases, the administrative unit, through still formally gazetted, is primarily ceremonial or informative with few or no administrative functions, such as <br /></p><p>- city, area, rural city, borough, town, <br /></p><p>- locality<br /></p><p>- shire, parish, hundred, division<br /></p><p>For any particular function it is expected that administrative units do not intersect or overlap. There will be a relationship with the government unit that is responsible for delegating the specific function, and there may be relationships with other administrative units. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Examples: <br /></p><p><a href="file://http://www.dtpli.vic.gov.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0013/111316/Melbourne_v47.pdf"><font color="#0000ff"><u>http://www.dtpli.vic.gov.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0013/111316/Melbourne_v47.pdf</u></font></a> <a href="file://http://www.health.vic.gov.au/regions/"><font color="#0000ff"><u>http://www.health.vic.gov.au/regions/</u></font></a> <a href="file://http://www.mfb.vic.gov.au/About-Us/StationMap.html"><font color="#0000ff"><u>http://www.mfb.vic.gov.au/About-Us/StationMap.html</u></font></a> and select ‘districts’ <a href="file://http://www.cfa.vic.gov.au/warnings-restrictions/find-your-fire-district/"><font color="#0000ff"><u>http://www.cfa.vic.gov.au/warnings-restrictions/find-your-fire-district/</u></font></a> <a href="file://http://vicwater.org.au/victorian-water-industry/catchment-management-authorities"><font color="#0000ff"><u>http://vicwater.org.au/victorian-water-industry/catchment-management-authorities</u></font></a> <a href="file://http://www.police.vic.gov.au/content.asp?Document_ID=7"><font color="#0000ff"><u>http://www.police.vic.gov.au/content.asp?Document_ID=7</u></font></a> <a href="file://http://melbourneschoolzones.com/"><font color="#0000ff"><u>http://melbourneschoolzones.com/</u></font></a> <br /></p><p>See also: <a href="file://http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cadastral_divisions_of_Australia"><font color="#0000ff"><u>http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cadastral_divisions_of_Australia</u></font></a> <br /></p><p><br /></p><p><br /></p>
