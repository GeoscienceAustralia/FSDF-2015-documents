-- Name --<br /></p><p>maritime zone<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>-- Definition --<br /></p><p>A belt of sea defined by international treaties and conventions, where coastal State executes jurisdictional rights.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>-- Description --<br /></p><p>NOTE 1 The zone is established for e.g. cadastral, administrative, economic, security or safety purposes. It is not established for environmental management or regulation, which is covered by the theme Area management/restriction/regulation zones and reporting areas, or for environmental protection, which is covered by the theme Protected sites.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>NOTE 2 The maritime zone can be either internal waters, territorial sea, contiguous zone, exclusive economic zone or continental shelf.<br /></p>
