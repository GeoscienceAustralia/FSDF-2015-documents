-- Definition --<br /></p><p>Unit of administration where a Government at some level has and/or exercises jurisdictional rights, for local, sub-national and national governance.<br /></p><p>-- Note --<br /></p><p>In Australia this corresponds with federal, state|territory, and local government units (LGAs). There is a strict 3-level hierarchy. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>The naming conventions for local governments vary across Australia. They are authorized by the state or territory government above them.  <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>--Cities (C)<br /></p><p>--Areas (A)<br /></p><p>--Rural Cities (RC)<br /></p><p>--Boroughs (B) <br /></p><p>--Shires (S)<br /></p><p>--Towns (T) <br /></p><p>--Regional Councils (R)<br /></p><p>--Municipalities/Municipal Councils (M)<br /></p><p>--District Councils (DC)<br /></p><p>--Regional Councils (RegC) <br /></p><p>--Aboriginal Councils (AC)<br /></p>
