<b>National Foundation Dataset:</b> A dataset that is aggregated or derived from component datasets and identified as a critical part of the national foundation framework. This dataset is focused on the national scale and there may/or may not be similar jurisdictional datasets held within each jurisdiction. Dtaaset are used as the basis for generating data products. A dataset in its entirety maybe delivered as a data product.
