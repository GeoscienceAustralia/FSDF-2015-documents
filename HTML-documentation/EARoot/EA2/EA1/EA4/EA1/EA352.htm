<b>Theme Working Group:  </b>Meetings Fortnightly (tele, email, in person as decided by working group). TWG is focused on  technical consultation, work plan implementation, coordination and ongoing evolution of the datasets. Duplication of effort between the FSDF and ICSM will be avoided by close ongoing engagement between groups. <b>ICSM</b>: There may be the ability for modifications to ICSM membership and reporting frameworks to undertake FSDF roles within some themes.<br /></p><p><i>Membership</i>: leadership group, data set custodians, key users, key suppliers, aggregators. Members may apply or be invited if users/suppliers or are invited if custodians/aggregators  <br /></p>
