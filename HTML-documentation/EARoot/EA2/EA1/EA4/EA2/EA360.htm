<b> for the FSDF: </b> An agency or organisation identified as being responsible for the development and management of a National Foundation Dataset, and who has the right to determine the condition applying to the use, distribution and composition of the National Foundation Dataset. The National Dataset Custodian would be responsible for the ongoing evolution of datasets as defined by itself and the thematic working groups. Their role would comprise the development and delivery of their National Foundation Dataset and the maintenance of a close working relationship with thematic sponsors to ensure that yearly vision statements are achieved.<br /></p><p>A National Dataset Custodian would be invited to the position by the chair of ANZLIC – The Spatial Information Council on behalf of the council.<br /></p><p><i>Contribution Expected:</i> 2 hours per week (Staff participation in working groups and documentation work). $15k each year for the FSDF technical evolution fund of the design level elements of the Framework. Technical evolution could include increased quality of data, increased efficiency in supply chains, modelling of datasets to better meet customer demands, standards implementation or new delivery mechanisms. <br /></p>
