<b>FSDF Secretariat:</b> Will provide ongoing coordination of the administrative elements of the FSDF. The secretariat will hold reporting templates, manage the FSDF Policy and Coordination Group and assist in the management of meetings of the TLG and TWG. The Secretariat will collate and deliver theme vision and update reports to the council. To avoid duplication the FSDF secretariat will be collaboration between OSP and ICSM to take advantage of established frameworks where possible.<br /></p><p><i>Contribution Expected:</i> Coordination and administration of the program. This will include facilitating leadership group meetings, managing the evolution fund, providing assistance to sponsors in the facilitation of working groups and managing the reporting framework. <br /></p>
