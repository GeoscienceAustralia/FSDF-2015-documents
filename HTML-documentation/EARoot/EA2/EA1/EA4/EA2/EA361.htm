An agency or organisation (Commonwealth or Jurisdictional) having a special interest in ensuring that the National Foundation Datasets within a particular theme are widely available and of high quality. The agency/organisation must have the structure and resources to enable it to provide leadership, in conjunction with the Office of Spatial Policy, to a number of National Dataset Custodians covering a range of National Foundation Datasets. Their role will include liaising with other national sponsors, consulting a community of users and coordinating activities with custodians to ensure foundation datasets are delivered and evolved in accordance with the wider ANZLIC direction and in conformance with standards, specifications and user priorities. <br /></p><p>A National Theme Sponsor would be invited to the position by the chair of ANZLIC – The Spatial Information Council on behalf of the council.<br /></p><p><i>Contribution Expected:</i> Each theme sponsor would be expected to provide an in-kind staff member to act as a working group facilitator. It is envisaged that this position would on average contribute 2 hours a week to the project.<br /></p>
