-- Definition --<br /></p><p>An hierarchical unit extends the unit definition with an hierarchal structure describing the topology of units (represents an individual element of that hierarchy). <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>In general, the root element starts with 'level=1' and has no higher units (meaning it is the parent) but possibly lower level units at 'level = 1 +n'.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>In reverse, a leaf element has 'level=maxLevel' with has no more lower units (as it is the leaf) but possibly higher level units with 'level=maxLevel-n'.<br /></p>
