-- Definition --<br /></p><p>UnitSpatialRelationship describes the 'topological' spatial relationship between defined units, e.g., adjacent, subsuming, intersecting, disjunctive etc. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>A spatial relationship has one or more source units and/or one or more target units. A defined unit knows about any spatial relationship they participate in. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p><br /></p><p><br /></p><p><br /></p><p><br /></p>
